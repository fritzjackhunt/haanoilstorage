Our Standards

Standardization is important for operational excellence. For this reason GSS Baltic Logistics has carefully recorded and analyzed its operational best practices around the world in order to develop 75 standards for global implementation.


These standards cover all aspects of a terminal's lifecycle in the fields of Safety, Health, Environment, Quality and Operations, as well as Technical and Project Management. These global standards, known as the Leading Way, are at the heart of GSS Baltic Logistics strategy and have been developed in close consultation with local technical and safety managers. We aims to bring about continuous improvement in every aspect of our operation.

Rotterdam Oil Storage Terminals

GSS Baltic Logistics operates highly sophisticated oil terminals strategically positioned in the commercial seaports of Houston and Rotterdam, serving as pivotal hubs in the realm of crude oil field logistics and storage. Our terminals are meticulously engineered, employing cutting-edge technology to ensure seamless intake of crude oil and oil products from rail tank cars, marine tankers, and pipelines.

Within each terminal, we have established our own advanced laboratory dedicated to the meticulous quality and quantity control of oil products. This rigorous testing process guarantees that every operation within our port, including bunkering and oil sites, is executed with precision and in strict adherence to the most stringent industry standards.

Our facilities feature storage tanks of varying capacities, meticulously designed to accommodate a diverse array of petroleum products, including Jet Fuel, Diesel Gasoil, and Ultra Low Sulfur. We have invested in advanced vapor generating capacity and a network of technological pipelines, facilitating swift and secure transportation of petroleum products between sites and tanks. This robust infrastructure enables us to efficiently deliver products to tankers and bunkering vessels, ensuring unparalleled reliability and efficiency.

Specializing in fuel oil and middle distillates, GSS Baltic Logistics boasts an impressive total capacity exceeding 418,000 cubic meters distributed across 18 meticulously maintained tanks. Our terminals handle a comprehensive range of products, including Diesel Gasoline, Naphtha, and Jet Fuel (K1, K2, and K3 categories). In addition to our advanced infrastructure, operational excellence is the hallmark of our service. Our terminals feature deep drafts and direct connections to truck, rail, and pipeline systems, guaranteeing swift and reliable distribution channels. At GSS Baltic Logistics, we are dedicated to delivering unparalleled solutions in crude oil logistics and storage, setting new benchmarks for excellence, professionalism, and efficiency in the industry.
